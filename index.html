Our Club

Dublin Dogos is the next big thing in Irish rugby. A team formed by expats and Irish players, playing together behind one jersey, the fierce Dogo!

Dublin Dogos will aim to be not just an ordinary club but a link between the Irish and the expats rugby community serving as a meeting point for expats and Irish people who love the sport of rugby, fostering integration and promoting social inclusion through this discipline.

We dream of a world without walls. We believe that no one should be an outsider. And that’s why Dublin Dogos was born, because we want to be the change we wish to see in the world translated into the sport we love the most.

Tournaments

For the opening ceremony of Dublin Dogos RFC we are organising a Tag Rugby blitz tournament at Coolmine Rugby Club, Ashtown, Dublin 15. There will be a huge argentinian BBQ and a South African Braai with all the meat coming from the magic fields of Ireland (with all the vegs for the non-meat eaters too!!), plus music and drinks in the club house, starting the matches at 11am and finishing with a late late party!

We still have space for a few teams but not that many and if you want to play but don't have a team, we will find a place for you. It doesn't matter if you haven't ever played tag rugby in your life. We will have a blast.

Entry is €120 for a team of 10 players (7 players + 3 subs).

It will be an amazing oportunity to get to know new people, have a few drinks, play if you are interested or just drop by for a good steak and a laugh!!
